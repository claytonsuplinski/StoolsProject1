CS 559 Project #1 README

-Keyboard Commands:
q, x, or ESC: Exits the program
e: Raises the seat of the stool
d: Lowers the seat of the stool
f: Toggles fog in and out of display
w: Toggles in and out of wireframe mode

KEY_UP: Rotates camera upward
KEY_DOWN: Rotates camera downward
KEY_LEFT: Rotates camera left
KEY_RIGHT: Rotates camera right


Bonus Features:
-The seat is able to adjust upward and back down based on the dimensions in the description. 
-Fog (moderately dense) can be added and removed from the scene.
-Basic local lighting aspects are implemented in the display.
